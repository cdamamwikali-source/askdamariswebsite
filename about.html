About Damaris Kakundi

Founder & Chief Career Strategist

DK

Damaris Kakundi

Career Development Expert & Mentor

With over a decade of experience in talent development and career coaching, Damaris Kakundi has dedicated her life to empowering African professionals to reach their full potential.

Having navigated her own career transformation from entry-level positions to executive leadership, Damaris understands firsthand the challenges and opportunities that African professionals face in today's global marketplace.

Her passion for bridging the gap between talent and opportunity led to the creation of Askdamaris, a platform that has helped hundreds of professionals transform their careers and achieve their dreams.

Our Mission

To empower African professionals with the skills, strategies, and confidence needed to compete successfully in the global job market and build fulfilling careers that create lasting impact.

Our Vision

A future where every talented African professional has access to the resources, guidance, and opportunities they need to achieve their career aspirations and contribute meaningfully to the global economy.

The Askdamaris Story

Askdamaris was born from a simple observation: talented African professionals often lack access to the career development resources, mentorship, and strategic guidance that could unlock their full potential.

After years of working in human resources and talent development, Damaris recognized a consistent pattern. Many professionals possessed incredible skills and potential but struggled to present themselves effectively, navigate career transitions, or access opportunities that matched their capabilities.

This realization sparked a mission to create a platform that would democratize access to world-class career development services, making them accessible and relevant to African professionals at all stages of their careers.

Today, Askdamaris serves professionals across Kenya and throughout Africa, providing personalized coaching, skills training, and career development services that have helped hundreds of individuals secure better positions, negotiate higher salaries, and build careers they're proud of.

Our approach is simple: we identify the gaps, provide the tools to bridge them, and support our clients every step of the way as they grow into the professionals they aspire to be.
